FROM THE NETHERLANDS TO PARADISE

Discover Italy’s Best-Kept Secret

Swap the Netherlands’ flat landscapes for a warm Alpine-Mediterranean valley in South Tyrol. With direct flights from Amsterdam to Verona or Innsbruck, you’re just a scenic drive away from Lagundo—the romantic hideaway near Merano.

Terraced vineyards blend with blooming apple orchards, and slender cypresses rise into deep blue skies. Wake to birdsong, sip crisp South Tyrolean wine on a panoramic, and watch the sunset ignite the mountains. Wander hand-in-hand along the historic Waalwege trails, enjoy alpine spa rituals, or simply unwind above Merano’s rooftops.

Still undiscovered by most Dutch travellers, Lagundo is Italy’s best-kept romantic secret—waiting for you between mountain peaks and Mediterranean gardens.

Wellness

Swim a few relaxed laps in our 34°C indoor pool, then drift in the outdoor pool while enjoying the surrounding mountain views. Our new wellness area invites you to unwind and recharge: enjoy the gentle warmth of the bio sauna, the views from the Finnish outdoor sauna, or relax in the aromatic steam bath. The whirlpool and quiet areas invite you to slow down, while treatments with alpine ingredients like arnica and mountain herbs leave you feeling refreshed.

Hike & Bike

Pedal straight from Heinrichshof into a sun-kissed network of riverside cycle paths, vineyard lanes, and Alpine trails. Free mountain bikes and e-bikes, GPS-guided tours, a secure bike garage, and a refreshing post-ride drink are all waiting for you. Step outside and you’ll also find a wide range of hiking trails starting right at the hotel, from gentle walks through orchards to panoramic mountain hikes.

Culinary

Wake to the aroma of freshly baked rye, mountain honey and espresso. Our Vital Breakfast presents orchard-picked apples, homemade jams, alpine yogurt, cold-smoked Speck, oven-warm croissants and eggs cooked à la minute. Evenings unfold into four Mediterranean-Tyrolean courses, salad bar and candlelight—flavourful proof that love truly travels through taste at Hotel Heinrichshof daily.

Your Perfect Vacation in the Italian Alps

Nestled where Italian sunshine meets Alpine freshness, Hotel Heinrichshof is your intimate hideaway above Merano. Awake to vineyard views, fuel up on orchard-fresh breakfasts, cycle scenic river paths, unwind in panoramic pools and savour candle-lit, Mediterranean-Tyrolean dinners. Whether you crave spa serenity or mountain adventure, Lagundo wraps each shared moment in effortless romance and quietly unforgettable South-Tyrolean charm.

Good to know for your stay at the Heinrichshof

Whether by air, rails or by road

By car:
Many guests from the Netherlands or Belgium prefer driving. From Utrecht, it’s about 1,000 km—mostly highway. Take the A22 Brenner motorway through Austria, exit at Bolzano-Sud, then continue on the MEBO expressway to the Lagundo exit. From there, it’s just a 5-minute drive to the hotel. Comfortable parking is available directly on site.

By plane:
Seasonal direct flights from Amsterdam to Verona or Innsbruck make air travel convenient. From the airport, it’s about a 2-hour scenic drive to Lagundo.

By train:
Travel via major hubs like Munich and continue to Merano. From Merano station, buses 213 or 235 take you to Lagundo in about 10 minutes.

At Hotel Heinrichshof, your day begins with our vital breakfast buffet (7:30–10:30 AM), served in the light-filled dining room or on the panoramic terrace. Enjoy freshly baked breads, homemade jams and cakes, fresh fruit, South Tyrolean Speck, cheeses, organic yogurt, muesli, eggs made to order, and a variety of teas and coffee specialties.

In the evening, indulge in our 5-course gourmet menu with a seasonal starter, soup, salad buffet, a choice of main courses, and a sweet dessert. The cuisine combines Mediterranean freshness with authentic South Tyrolean flavors—light, regional, and full of variety.
