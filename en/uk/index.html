ITALY‘S HIDDEN GEM IN THE ALPS - JUST ONE FLIGHT AWAY

Escape the UK’s grey skies for Alpine sunshine in just a few hours. With direct SkyAlps flights from London to Bolzano (BZO), or smooth connections via Verona or Munich, South Tyrol is closer than you think. From Bolzano Airport, it’s only a 30-minute scenic drive to Hotel Der Heinrichshof in Algund.

Wake up to birdsong and panoramic views. Sip South Tyrolean wine on a sunlit terrace framed by roses and cypress trees. Hike peaceful trails like the Waalwege, relax in the panoramic sauna, or simply slow down above Merano’s rooftops.

Still a well-kept secret for most UK travellers, Algund blends Italian charm, Alpine character, and heartfelt hospitality. It's the perfect place to switch off and feel genuinely far away — without the long-haul flight.

Wellness

Swim a few relaxed laps in our 34°C indoor pool, then drift in the outdoor pool while enjoying the surrounding mountain views. Our new wellness area invites you to unwind and recharge: enjoy the gentle warmth of the bio sauna, the views from the Finnish outdoor sauna, or relax in the aromatic steam bath. The whirlpool and quiet areas invite you to slow down, while treatments with alpine ingredients like arnica and mountain herbs leave you feeling refreshed.

Hike & Bike

Pedal straight from Heinrichshof into a sun-kissed network of riverside cycle paths, vineyard lanes, and Alpine trails. Free mountain bikes and e-bikes, GPS-guided tours, a secure bike garage, and a refreshing post-ride drink are all waiting for you. Step outside and you’ll also find a wide range of hiking trails starting right at the hotel, from gentle walks through orchards to panoramic mountain hikes.

Culinary

Wake to the aroma of freshly baked rye, mountain honey and espresso. Our Vital Breakfast presents orchard-picked apples, homemade jams, alpine yogurt, cold-smoked Speck, oven-warm croissants and eggs cooked à la minute. Evenings unfold into four Mediterranean-Tyrolean courses, salad bar and candlelight—flavourful proof that love truly travels through taste at Hotel Heinrichshof daily.

Good to know for your stay at the Heinrichshof

Whether by air, rails or by road

Direct SkyAlps flights connect London directly with Bolzano (BZO); smooth connections are also available via Verona or Munich.

From Bolzano Airport it’s only a 30-minute scenic drive to Hotel Der Heinrichshof in Algund.

Prefer rails? Travel via Munich to Merano, then hop on local buses 213 or 235; they reach Algund in roughly 10 minutes.

Drivers follow the A22 Brenner motorway, leave at Bolzano-Sud, take the MEBO expressway and slip road “Algund” for the final five minutes to the hotel.

At Hotel Heinrichshof, your day begins with our vital breakfast buffet (7:30–10:30 AM), served in the light-filled dining room or on the panoramic terrace. Enjoy freshly baked breads, homemade jams and cakes, fresh fruit, South Tyrolean Speck, cheeses, organic yogurt, muesli, eggs made to order, and a variety of teas and coffee specialties.

In the evening, indulge in our 5-course gourmet menu with a seasonal starter, soup, salad buffet, a choice of main courses, and a sweet dessert. The cuisine combines Mediterranean freshness with authentic South Tyrolean flavors—light, regional, and full of variety.
